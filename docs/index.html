About the BOLDC Open Lab

Inviting the outside world into the classroom and bringing the classroom to the outside world defines the open lab experience.

Thanks to the Black family’s generosity, The School of Humanities and Social Sciences has established the Behrend Open Laboratory for Digital Cinema (BOLD Cinema). We view cinema as essential to exploring human experience, and we wish to encourage creative cinematic collaboration between Behrend and the local community.

BOLD Cinema encourages collaborations of PSU Behrend students with local independent filmmakers, non-profit institutions, and commercial clients. We are eager to support boldly experimental cinematic projects with our students and the Erie community at large.
