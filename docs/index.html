The Behrend Open Lab for Digital Cinema

at Penn State Erie, The Behrend College

Thanks to the Black familyâ€™s generosity, The School of Humanities and Social Sciences has established the Behrend Open Laboratory for Digital Cinema (BOLD Cinema). We view cinema as essential to exploring human experience, and we wish to encourage creative cinematic collaboration between Behrend and the local community.

Inviting the outside world into the classroom and bringing the classroom to the outside world defines the open lab experience. BOLD Cinema encourages collaborations of PSU Behrend students with local independent filmmakers, non-profit institutions, and commercial clients. We are eager to support boldly experimental cinematic projects with our students and the Erie community at large.
